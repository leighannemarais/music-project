Why Ed Sheeran

Why is Ed Sheeran the best?

Sheeran's musical style has been described as pop, folk-pop, and soft rock. He also incorporates rap into his music. Sheeran has sold more than 150 million records worldwide, making him one of the world's best-selling music artists. He has 101 million RIAA-certified units in the US, and two of his albums are in the list of the best-selling albums in UK chart history. In December 2019, the Official Charts Company named him artist of the decade, with the most combined success in the UK album and singles charts in the 2010s. As of April 2022, he is the most followed artist on Spotify. Beginning in March 2017, his รท Tour became the highest-grossing of all time in August 2019.

One of the most amazing things about Ed, is that he really is an amazing musician. Every single sound created, is one done by him with his feet and a loop pedal. Just give Ed a guitar and a loop pedal and he will be able to make his songs sound amazing. The set-up process with the looping is so methodical and it is amazing to watch at his concerts.

Beautiful People

"Beautiful People" is a song by English singer-songwriter Ed Sheeran featuring American singer Khalid. It was released through Asylum Records as the third single from the former's compilation album, No.6 Collaborations Project (2019). It reached number one on the UK Singles Chart in July 2019, becoming the second single from the album to reach the summit of the chart.

Bloodstream

"Bloodstream" was written by Sheeran, Rudimental, and Snow Patrol members; Johnny McDaid and Gary Lightbody, and produced by Rick Rubin at Shangri-La in Malibu, California. The song is about Sheeran taking MDMA during a wedding celebration in Ibiza. He claims that during the experience he "fell in love with a beanbag" and that "he felt anxiety, felt love, felt warm and he felt a bit weird"
